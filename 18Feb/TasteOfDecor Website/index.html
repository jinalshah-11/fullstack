About Us

Interior

We Design, Deliver Inspirational, Personal Interiors, Created for each Individual Client. We are proud not to have a “house style”, our Co-founder and Design Director, works closely with each of our clients, taking great time and care to discover and nurture their aesthetic and lifestyle aspirations for their project.

Exterior

A well designed garden area complements the interior and architectural features of the property, often with the interior aesthetic being carried through into the outside space, blurring the boundary between inside and out.

Design

All our design and styling services are offered as stand alone services to clients who wish to undertake the construction and project management works themselves. However, Taste of Decor is unique in that we offer a complete, in house construction and project management service, from concept to completion.

Decoration

Bespoke fitted and freestanding joinery designed for a space, are often key pieces of a scheme. Bespoke pieces can elevate a space either through their aesthetic qualities, using unusual materials and textures or by creating practical clever design solutions for difficult areas.

Planning

Space planning is a fundamental element of the Our design process. The designer then draws up a plan that defines the zones of the space and the activities that will take place in those zones. The space plan will also define the circulation patterns that show how people will move through the space.

Execution

The moment when the design comes to life and when you witness the transformation of the client's home. It is up to Taste of Decor to make sure the final outcome is perfect and according to the vision shared by the client and themselves.
